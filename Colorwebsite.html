My Favorite Color, Green

The reason my favorite color is green is because it is a natural comforting color. The other colors of this website fit well with green because they are also natural, and remind me of nature, and forests.

Color schemes are used in websites to create a visually appealing place for the users eyes to rest. Without color schemes that are paired well the users would be looking at harshly contrating colors, and this can become severely unpleasent, especially if it interferes with the readability of the site. Along with this, pleasant color schemes can cause users to feel comforted or happy, and this can lead to longer user time on the websites, as well as more reaccuring visits.
